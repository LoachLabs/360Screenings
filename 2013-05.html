One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest

"I must be crazy to be in a loony bin like this." - McMurphy

360 Screenings presented ONE FLEW OVER THE CUCKOO'S NEST on May 31st & June 1st, 2013 to a record breaking crowd! Over 500 'patients' filled the rooms and hallways of the historic Christie Mansion, taking part in the various entrance examinations.

View the picture gallery.

The night began with a brief interview with Doctor Spivey who analyzed the current state of each and every guest. Careful attention was paid, and should there have been cause for further investigation, it was brought up with Nurse Ratched in the group therapy wing.

Once cleared, patients were given access to the facilities and were free to explore the state of the art electroshock therapy equipment. Spontaneous checkups were made to ensure all guests were properly medicated with the appropriate dosage, and calisthenics were offered to those looking to strengthen muscles, build endurance and improve mental clarity.

Few patients challenged authority, but those that did managed to escape to Canada. There, they found peace of mind.

To close the evening, all patients were given a box of either Oreos or Chips Ahoy! cookies as a parting gift, and as a way of honouring Mr. Christie's first home and historic significance to the city of Toronto.

We would like to thank everyone for making this screening an insanely good time!

Christie Mansion
